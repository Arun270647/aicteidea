AICTE IDEA LAB

EVENTS

The AICTE IDEA Lab regularly organizes various events, workshops, and programs to foster innovation, enhance technical skills, and promote entrepreneurship among students and faculty. These events are designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

ANNUAL EVENTS CALENDAR

Collaboration with Industry

Collaborations with industry partners to showcase lab projects, gain industry insights, and network with potential clients. Address real-time issues through industry collaboration. Expertise gained through internships will be tested and prototyped at IDEA Lab.

Topics Covered:
  • To address real-time issues.
  • Expertise obtained through internships will be tested.
  • Expertise obtained through internships will be prototyped at IDEA Lab.

Extension of Academic Expertise

Extend the services of academic experts to train students and professors from other schools using the expertise gained.

Topics Covered:
  • To extend the services of academic experts.
  • To train students using the expertise gained.
  • To train professors from other schools using the expertise gained.

Angel Investor Network

Build an angel investor network to support ideas from the IDEA Lab.

Topics Covered:
  • To build an angel investor network.
  • To support ideas from the IDEA Lab.

Skill Enhancement
