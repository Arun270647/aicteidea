AICTE IDEA LAB

EVENTS

The AICTE IDEA Lab regularly organizes various events, workshops, and programs to foster innovation, enhance technical skills, and promote entrepreneurship among students and faculty. These events are designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

ANNUAL EVENTS CALENDAR

Faculty Development Programs (FDP)

3 per year

Comprehensive training programs designed to enhance faculty skills in emerging technologies and teaching methodologies.

Topics Covered:
  • Advanced AI & Machine Learning Techniques
  • Industry 4.0 & Smart Manufacturing
  • Digital Transformation in Education
  • Research Methodology & Publication

Technical Bootcamps

3 per year

Intensive hands-on training programs for students to master cutting-edge technologies and tools.

Bootcamp Tracks:
  • Full Stack Web Development
  • Mobile App Development (Android/iOS)
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps

Ideation Workshops

3 per year

Creative problem-solving sessions focused on generating innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Workshop Focus Areas:
  • Design Thinking Methodology
  • Startup Idea Generation
  • Social Innovation Projects
  • Sustainable Technology Solutions

Annual Conference & Symposium

1 per year

Premier academic and industry conference bringing together researchers, industry experts, and students.

Conference Highlights:
  • Keynote Speeches by Industry Leaders
  • Research Paper Presentations
  • Industry Panel Discussions
  • Student Project Exhibitions
  • Startup Pitch Competitions

School Student Programs

6 per year

Technical skill development workshops specifically designed for school and polytechnic students.

Program Components:
  • Basic Programming & Coding
  • Robotics & Electronics
  • 3D Printing & Design
  • Science Project Development

School Teacher Awareness Programs

2 per year

Professional development programs for school teachers to integrate technology in their teaching methods.

Training Areas:
  • Digital Teaching Tools
  • STEM Education Methods
  • Educational Technology Integration
  • Student Assessment Techniques

Open Day for School Students

1 per year

Annual open house event showcasing IDEA Lab facilities and opportunities to inspire young minds.

Open Day Activities:
  • Lab Tours & Equipment Demonstrations
  • Interactive Science Experiments
  • Student Project Showcases
  • Career Guidance Sessions

Hands-on Skill Training Programs

4 per year

Practical training sessions focused on developing technical skills through real-world projects.

Training Modules:
  • Arduino & Raspberry Pi Programming
  • 3D Modeling & Printing
  • IoT Device Development
  • Mobile App Development
