VISTAS AICTE IDEA LAB

VISION & MISSION

VISION

  • To achieve excellence in product creation and innovation, resulting in start-ups by developing passionate entrepreneurs.
  • To prepare the next generation of young brains to turn their unique ideas into profitable products.
  • To support effective lab utilization in rural and urban areas near the institution.

MISSION

  • Product Deployment in Existing Infrastructure: Provide state-of-the-art facilities and resources to nurture creative thinking and innovative problem-solving capabilities among students and faculty.
  • Knowledge and Expertise Sharing: Foster collaborative partnerships with industry leaders to ensure practical relevance and real-world application of academic research.
  • Collaboration with Fabrication Lab: Equip students with 21st-century skills including critical thinking, design thinking, and entrepreneurial mindset to become technology leaders.
  • Youth Empowerment Through Access: Support the development of innovative ideas into viable business ventures through mentorship, resources, and strategic guidance.

CORE OBJECTIVES

Foster Innovation and Creative Thinking

To cultivate a culture of innovation by encouraging students and faculty to explore, experiment, and develop original solutions through hands-on projects and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Strengthen Industry-Academia Collaboration

To bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industrial needs by providing real-world exposure, problem-solving opportunities, and joint project development with industry partners.
